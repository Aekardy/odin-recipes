CHICKEN AND EGG CURRY

Description

This flavourful dish combines chicken, egg, carrots, chillies, tomatoes, onions, potatoes, and spinach into a hearty cup. This also offers a delightful blend of protein, veegetables, and spices, making it a satisfying meal!


Ingredients

  • 200g chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 eggs, hard-boiled and halved
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2-3 green chillies,, finely chopped (adjust to spice preferences)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 potato, peeled and diced
  • A handful of spinach leaves, washed and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon corriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder (adjust to spice preferences)
  • Salt to taste
  • For garnishing: Fresh cilantro leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add the chopped onion and saute it until tanslucent.
  3. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and saute for another 2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn white or are no longer pink.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  6. Stir in the coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the chicken.
  7. Add the sliced carrots, diced potatoes, and chopped green chillies. Stir to combine.
  8. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients, and let it simmer on medium-low heat until the chicken is cooked through, and the vegetables are tender. This may take about 20-25 minutes.
  9. Once the curry has a thick consistency, gently place the halved hard-boiled eggs into the curry.
  10. Add the chopped spinach leaves and let them wilt into the curry.
  11. Garning with fresh cilantro leaves.
  12. Serve the chicken and egg curry hot with steamed rice or bread of choice.
