STUFFED TOMATOES WITH EGG AND POTATOES

Description

Need a flavourful and unique dish? How about stuffed tomatoes with eggs and potatoes? This dish combines the ingredients mentioned below into a saisfying single dish. Enjoy your meal!


Ingredients

  • Large tomatoes (as many as needed!)
  • Eggs (same number as tomatoes)
  • Potatoes (half the number of tomatoes, peeled and diced)
  • Onions (half the number of potatoes, finely chopped)
  • 3 (or more as needed) tablespoons oil (vegetable or olive oil)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For garnishing: Fresh herbs and additional seasonings as preferred

Instructions

  1. Cut the tops off the tomatoes and carefully scoop out the pulp and seeds to create a hollow space inside. Reserve the pulp for later.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and diced potatoes. Saute until the potatoes are golden and tender, about 10-15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. In the meantime beat 1/4th of the eggs in a bowl and add the reserved tomato pulp. Mix well.
  4. Once the potatoes and onions are cooked, mix them with the egg and tomato pulp mixture.
  5. Carefully fill each hollowed out tomato with the egg, potato and onion mixture.
  6. Heat remaining oil in the same skillet over medium heat. Place the stuffed tomatoes in the skillet.
  7. Cover the skillet and cook the stuffed tomatoes for about 15-20 minutes, or until the tomatoes are tender, and the egg filling is set. You may need to rotate the tomatoes gently to ensure even cooking.
  8. While the stuffed tomatoes are cooking, fry the remaining eggs in a separate pan to desired level of doneness.
  9. Once the stuffed tomatoes are done, remove them from the skillet and place a fried egg on top of each one.
  10. Garninsh with fresh herbs or additional seasonings if desired, and serve hot!
